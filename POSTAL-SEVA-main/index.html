About Us

Empowering India's Post Offices with Smart Feedback Systems

In today's fast-paced world, India Post continues to be a cornerstone of communication and service delivery. Yet, delays, inefficiencies, and outdated feedback loops can slow progress.

Postal Seva modernizes this ecosystem by offering real-time, multi-platform feedback solutions—through WhatsApp bots, call bots, QR codes, and web forms.

Each feedback is securely linked to a unique Post Office ID and visualized on a live dashboard, helping postal leadership act promptly and improve service quality across the country.
